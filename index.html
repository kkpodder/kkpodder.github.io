01 — About

I am a PhD researcher working at the intersection of machine learning, biomedical signal processing, robotics, and healthcare AI.

My goal is to develop data-driven methods that are robust, interpretable, and directly applicable to scientific and clinical problems. I believe rigorous engineering and human-centered design are inseparable.
