01 — Research Interests
◈

Continual & Lifelong Learning

Replay-based and uncertainty-aware frameworks that let robots learn new tasks without forgetting prior knowledge under distribution shift.

◈

Computer Vision & Perception

Video-based gesture recognition, masked autoencoders for self-supervised learning, and multimodal perception from visual and sensor streams.

◈

Robotics & IoT Systems

Deploying adaptive perception models on physical robots and smart IoT agents — bridging sim-to-real gaps in real-world environments.

◈

Biomedical AI

Deep learning for biosignal analysis, medical imaging, and transcranial Doppler ultrasound — translating models toward clinical utility.

◈

Human–Robot Interaction

Trustworthy hand-signal communication between humans and robotic agents, with conformal prediction for uncertainty-aware decision making.
