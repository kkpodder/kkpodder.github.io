BibTeX → publications.json

Convert your Google Scholar export into a JSON file for your website. Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Step 01

Export from Google Scholar

Go to your Google Scholar profile, click Export (below your name), select BibTeX, and choose Export all. Open the downloaded file in any text editor, select all, and paste below.

Step 02

Paste your BibTeX

Step 03

Download publications.json

Place publications.json in the root of your GitHub Pages repo alongside index.html. Your website will load it automatically.

Step 04

Drop it in your repo

Put publications.json in your repo root (same folder as index.html). Commit and push. Your publications page will update instantly. Next time you publish, just re-export from Scholar and repeat from Step 01 — takes about 30 seconds.
